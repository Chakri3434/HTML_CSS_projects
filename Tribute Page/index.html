As the Founder and CEO of GeeksforGeeks, Sandeep Jain has been leading one of the largest and most trusted online platforms for learning and practicing computer science concepts. With over 14 years of experience in the ed-tech industry, he has a passion for teaching and mentoring students and professionals who aspire to excel in the tech world.

About

Sandeep's journey started from a humble background and a tier-3 engineering college, where he faced many challenges and insecurities to achieve his dream job. After cracking IIT and working for a top US-based software company, he realized the need to help millions of students who struggle with the same issues. He quit his lucrative tech job and started GeeksforGeeks as a blog in 2008, with the intention of helping engineering students prepare for placements. Since then, he have curated and taught courses like DSA and CIP, and helped thousands of students get placed in tech giants like Amazon, Google, Meta, Apple, Microsoft, and many more.
